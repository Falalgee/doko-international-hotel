Doko International Hotel opened its doors in 1998 with a vision to redefine hospitality in Niger State. Over two decades later, we remain a family‑run establishment where every guest is treated with genuine warmth and attention to detail.

Our design blends contemporary comfort with subtle nods to local heritage — from hand‑crafted furnishings to curated art. Whether you’re here for business, a family gathering, or a peaceful retreat, we create an atmosphere of understated luxury.
